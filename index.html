why ferb exists

i built ferb because i needed it.

i built ferb out of my own frustration with how often things almost came together but didn't, not because i lacked ideas or direction, but because nothing was holding the work in motion. i'd start strong, lose context, get pulled into other threads, and slowly drift away from what actually mattered.

over time, i realized i didn't need more tools or better thinking. i needed something that stayed with the work, kept things aligned, and helped me follow through.

ferb is my attempt at building that layer.
